## Tips

1. **Start small**: Begin with a simple version, iterate later
2. **Solve your own problems**: Best extensions solve real pain points
3. **Check existing extensions**: Avoid duplicating what exists
4. **Get feedback early**: Share prototypes with teammates
5. **Keep it focused**: One extension, one job, done well
